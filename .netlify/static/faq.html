Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about setting up and running Oyik's AI agents.

•Does it work on Instagram/Facebook/WhatsApp?

Yes—your AI can respond where your leads already message, and keep the same qualification + booking flow.

•Can it show property photos and details in chat?

Yes—your chatbot can share photos and listing details directly in the conversation.

•Can it book viewings automatically?

Yes—when connected to your calendar, it can check availability and schedule viewings, then send confirmations.

•What if someone wants a human?

The agent can transfer chats/calls to your sales team instantly.

•How do you qualify leads?

You choose the questions (budget, move-in date, smoking/pets, employment, household size, etc.) and we configure them.

•Is it customizable to our brand voice?

Yes—tone, scripts, FAQs, and templates are configured to match your agency.

•How long does setup take?

Typical setup is days—not months—depending on channels, calendar, and data sources.

•Do you support SMS/email/WhatsApp reminders?

Yes—reminders can be sent via the channels you choose, including voice calls if needed.
